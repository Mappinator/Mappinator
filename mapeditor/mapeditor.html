Tips for use

Drop a file to open it.

Press 'D' for 'draw' mode


Press 'F' for 'fold' mode, click on the crease to change fold type.


Press 'M' for 'modify(scale)' mode, type numbers or dot(.) to change scale rate,

click on the crease to scale, the rate is under SQRT. (Press 'C' to clear)


Press 'A' for 'angle' mode, which is designed for accurate drawing,

click on some dot, then another one to measure the angle. The

accurate angle can be seen in the top-right corner (in degrees).

(Press 'Q' to add 90 d to it, Press 'H' to obtain half of it,

Press 'T' to obtain one-third of it, Press 'C' to clear)

When an angle is measured, in 'draw' mode you will be quite easy to

draw a crease parallel to it. Just click on a dot and you will get it.


Press 'U' for 'unit' mode, for more convenient drawing. (It can be measured like angle)

It decides the 'unit' length on two directions, horizontal and vertical.

('H' for half, 'T' for one-third, 'R' to reverse x and y, 'C' to recover)


Press 'E' for 'erase' mode.

Press 'S' to save it.

Press 'P' to save it as a SVG picture.

Press 'O' to output it, with convexs decomposition.
